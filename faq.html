What is the difference between buttercream and fondant?

French buttercream (as the name states) is made with lots of butter, which gives it a creamy texture and an off-white color. Fondant is a special type of icing which is rolled out and then draped over the cake.
Fondant is also used to create edible decorations such as bows, flowers, figurines, etc.
